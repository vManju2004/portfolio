ABOUT

Profile

I'm a final-year M.Tech (Integrated) student in Data Science at VIT Vellore, spending most of my time getting models from "it runs" to "it's production-grade." That's meant federated CNN-LSTMs for IoT security, fine-tuned CNNs for crop disease, and ensemble pipelines for agricultural forecasting — usually landing in the high 90s on accuracy, and always shipped behind an API rather than left in a notebook.

I work mainly in Python with TensorFlow and PyTorch, and I'm especially interested in federated learning — training strong models without ever centralizing the raw data.
